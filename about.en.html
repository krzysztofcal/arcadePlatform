About

Arcade Hub is a browser gaming portal focused on fast-loading HTML5 arcade games, lightweight player accounts, and a shared XP system across supported titles.

We build and maintain the project as an independent web platform under KCSWH, with an emphasis on simple controls, mobile-friendly layouts, and clear account and privacy settings.

Contact: contact@kcswh.pl
