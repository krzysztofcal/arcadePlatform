About Arcade Hub

Arcade Hub is an independent browser gaming project operated by KCSWH. We publish quick-play HTML5 games, maintain player-facing portal pages, and support account features such as XP sync and optional sign-in.

The platform is designed to work directly in the browser on desktop and mobile devices. We focus on short sessions, accessible controls, and clear information about privacy, cookies, and account management.

What you can do on the site

Visitors can browse and play arcade titles in the main portal, use optional accounts for cloud-synced progress, and access multiplayer gameplay features built around virtual-chip poker and community play inside the wider Arcade Hub service.

How the platform works

Arcade Hub groups several browser-based games and account tools under one shared interface. The service includes navigation between games, optional sign-in, synced XP, favorites, recently played history, and profile-related pages that help players continue across sessions.

We aim to keep the product lightweight and accessible. Most pages are intended to load directly in the browser without separate installation steps, and support materials remain publicly available so visitors can understand the project before signing in.

Who operates the site

KCSWH operates Arcade Hub as an independent web project. We maintain the site content, platform code, account systems, and public support materials published on this domain and on play.kcswh.pl.
