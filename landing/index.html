Arcade Hub by KCSWH

Arcade Hub is a browser-based gaming project operated by KCSWH. The platform brings together quick-play arcade titles, optional player accounts, cloud-synced XP, favorites, recently played history, and multiplayer features such as poker with virtual chips.

The goal of the site is to offer lightweight entertainment that works directly in the browser on desktop and mobile devices without requiring an install. Players can open games quickly, track progress across sessions, and return to the same profile from multiple devices.

KCSWH maintains the platform, publishes policy and support information, and develops the account, gameplay, and community systems used by Arcade Hub. The project is presented as a standalone entertainment site with its own public pages, contact channel, and privacy information.

Quick browser play
Arcade Hub is built around short, direct sessions. Games open in the browser, work on common desktop and mobile setups, and are designed for immediate play.
Accounts and XP
Users can create optional accounts to sync XP, keep a personal profile, review recent activity, and maintain continuity between visits.
Game catalog
The platform combines classic-style arcade games, puzzle experiences, and card-based features inside one shared portal with consistent navigation and account features.
Virtual-chip poker
Poker is presented as an in-platform multiplayer feature with virtual chips, account-linked activity, and browser-based table access inside the wider Arcade Hub service.
