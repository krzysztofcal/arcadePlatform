Who we are

We are KCSWH (“we”, “us”, “our”) and we operate the kcswh.pl landing site and associated contact forms. You can reach us at contact@kcswh.pl.

Personal data we process

  • Server logs. Our hosting provider records technical information (IP address, browser, timestamps) to keep the service reliable and secure.
  • Contact messages. If you email us we receive the data you provide (name, email, message) to reply to you.
  • Consent information. When presented with a consent banner we store your selections so we can honour them.

How we use this information

  • To deliver the website and prevent abuse or fraud.
  • To respond to enquiries you voluntarily send to us.
  • To meet our legal obligations under EU/UK privacy law.

Third parties and international transfers

We rely on trusted service providers (such as hosting and email providers) who may process data in the European Union or other countries with appropriate safeguards in place (e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses).

Your rights

You have the right to request access, correction or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, or to withdraw consent, contact us at contact@kcswh.pl.

Cookies and ads

Some pages embed advertising or analytics technologies that set cookies only after you grant consent. Use the “Manage cookies” control to review or change your choices at any time.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will post a new version on this page with an updated date when changes are made.
