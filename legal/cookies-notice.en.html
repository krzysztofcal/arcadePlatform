Cookies Notice

Last updated: 2025‑05‑06

How we use cookies

Cookies and similar technologies help run the Arcade Hub portal, secure Supabase authentication, and measure usage when you consent. You can change your choices anytime via Cookiebot.

Cookie categories

  • Strictly necessary: Supabase authentication cookies, session tokens, and basic preferences that keep you signed in and protect your account. Required for accounts and cannot be disabled without turning off sign-in.
  • Preferences: Stores your language and consent choices so the site remembers your settings.
  • Statistics/Analytics: Google Analytics (GA4) loads only after you grant consent in Cookiebot.

Supabase cookies

Supabase issues secure cookies to manage sessions and email verification. They are set as necessary cookies and are processed under the protections described in our Privacy Policy and the Supabase Data Processing Agreement.

Managing your choices

  • Open the Manage cookies control to update consent at any time.
  • You can also clear cookies in your browser settings; this may sign you out.

More information

For details on data use, transfers, and your rights, read our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
