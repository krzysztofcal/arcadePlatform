About the service

Arcade Hub is a browser arcade operated by KCSWH, offering casual games, an XP system, and an optional user account for cloud sync powered by Supabase. You may play as a guest or sign in to save XP and progress.

Using Arcade Hub

  • You must provide accurate email information if you create an account and keep credentials secure.
  • Guest mode is available but does not sync XP between devices.
  • Accounts are optional; deleting your account will stop cloud sync and remove associated data as described in the Privacy Policy.

XP and cloud sync

  • XP earned while signed in is linked to your user ID and stored with Supabase.
  • We may adjust XP in cases of abuse, cheating, or technical errors.
  • Connectivity issues may delay sync; we aim to retry without losing progress.
